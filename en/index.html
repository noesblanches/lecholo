A Sanctuary of Serenity

Surrounded by whispering trees and the gentle hum of nature, **Le Cholo** is a place where time slows, and peace prevails.

Set on an **expansive 30-acre estate**, this **Breton manor house** invites you to escape the ordinary and embrace the beauty of the wild.

The Heart of Authentic Brittany

From rugged coastlines to medieval towns, the world around Le Cholo is rich with wonder:

  • 🌊 **Golden beaches**, kissed by the rolling Atlantic waves.
  • 🌿 **Mystical forests and hidden trails**, perfect for peaceful walks.
  • 🏰 **Charming villages like Vannes and Pont-Aven**, where history comes alive.
  • 🥖 **Bustling markets**, brimming with fresh seafood and local delicacies.

An Unforgettable Stay

Whether you seek a **romantic hideaway, a family retreat,** or a simple moment to reconnect with nature, **Le Cholo is waiting for you**.
